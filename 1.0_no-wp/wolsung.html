Wolsung

These are the extraordinary times of Magical-Industrial Revolution. In Lyonesse, the city of cities, the sky is crowded with airships and steam-powered automobiles race the streets.

Elven aristocrats from their gentelmen's clubs plot intricate intrigues. Workshops and factories spawn mad scientists' mechanical golems and cabalistic thinking machines. Halfling mobsters and orc triads fight their war in backalleys and docks. In the dark corners of the city, unnoticed by the warring clubs, the undead rise their heads planning to bring back horrors of the Great War.

These daring times demand extraordinary heroes.  And sometimes even ‘extraordinary’ is just not enough!

About

Wolsung is a 28mm miniature skirmish game set in the Fantastic 19th Century!

The game is very quick to learn and plays beautifully with more than 2 players. All the models are very characterful and cinematic, and their rules represent that on the battlefield!

The game can be played on as small a surface as 2ft by 2ft and is designed to not ever require more than 3ft by 3ft.

The rules were designed with multiplayer games in mind and will never keep anyone waiting. As long as you have a hand of cards you can influence the situation on the battlefield. But, cards are just an addition, normal shooting and fighting is resolved just with a number of 6-sided dice!

Miniatures

You can buy Wolsung miniatures in either white metal or resin. The white metal models are cast for at Griffin Moulds Ltd in England, whereas the resin models are cast at Micro Art Studio in Poland.

Due to the location of products, it enabled Microart to ensure that quality control is very tight.

Scenery

Wolsung scenery and terrain is made from HDF (high density fibreboard) and manufactured in Poland.

Most people are more familiar with MDF; while HDF is very similar, it main benefit over MDF is that it is a stronger and harder product than MDF due to the fact it has a higher density.
